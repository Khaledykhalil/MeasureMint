MeasureMint Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 31, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how MeasureMint ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Miro application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Provided by Miro

When you use MeasureMint through Miro, we may receive:

  • Your Miro user ID
  • Board information (board ID, board content that you explicitly interact with)
  • Team information (if applicable)

2.2 Usage Data

We collect information about how you use the App, including:

  • Measurement data you create (calibrations, distances measured)
  • Unit preferences (feet/inches, meters, etc.)
  • Dark mode preferences
  • Error logs and diagnostic information

2.3 What We DON'T Collect

We do NOT collect, store, or access:

  • Your personal information (name, email, phone number) beyond what Miro provides
  • Content from your Miro boards that you don't explicitly interact with
  • Payment information (handled by Miro)
  • Location data
  • Your images, blueprints, or floor plans (measurements are overlays only)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain MeasureMint's measurement functionality
  • Process your calibrations and distance measurements
  • Save your unit and display preferences for convenience
  • Improve the App based on usage patterns
  • Debug and fix technical issues
  • Ensure security and prevent abuse

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Storage

All measurement data is stored as objects on your Miro board (lines, text labels, shapes). User preferences are stored in your browser's local storage. We do not maintain separate databases of your personal information or board content.

4.2 Security Measures

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • OAuth 2.0 authentication through Miro's secure platform
  • We follow Miro's security guidelines and best practices
  • Access tokens are securely stored and never shared
  • We regularly update dependencies to address security vulnerabilities

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your data with third parties except:

  • Miro Platform: MeasureMint operates on Miro's platform and is subject to Miro's Privacy Policy
  • Service Providers: We may use trusted service providers (hosting) who are bound by confidentiality agreements
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about data we hold about you
  • Correction: Measurements can be updated or corrected directly in the app
  • Deletion: Delete measurements by removing objects from your Miro board
  • Portability: Export measurement data using the export feature
  • Objection: Object to certain data processing activities
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time by uninstalling the App

7. Children's Privacy

MeasureMint is not intended for use by children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 16.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

9. Contact Us

For questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us:

  • GitHub: github.com/Khaledykhalil/MeasureMint
  • Issues: Report privacy concerns

By using MeasureMint, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
